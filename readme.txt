== Description ==

**AI-Powered Content Creation for WordPress.** [Visit our website](https://genwave.ai/)

Gen Wave brings the power of AI to your WordPress site. Create product descriptions, blog posts, images, and more - without any technical setup. No API keys required.

This free plugin connects your site to the Gen Wave platform and includes basic AI content generation. For bulk operations and advanced features, upgrade to [Gen Wave Pro](https://genwave.ai/).

= What's Included (Free) =

* **Account Connection** – Securely connect to your Gen Wave account
* **Single Content Generation** – Generate AI content for individual posts/products
* **Token Balance Tracking** – Monitor your usage and remaining tokens
* **Multi-language Support** – Generate content in English, Hebrew, Arabic, Russian, French, and Chinese

= Gen Wave Pro Features =

Upgrade to Pro to unlock powerful bulk features:

* **Bulk Product Descriptions** – Generate WooCommerce descriptions for hundreds of products at once
* **Bulk Blog Posts** – Create multiple posts automatically in any supported language
* **AI Image Generation** – Generate and insert images directly to your media library
* **SEO Analysis** – Scan your entire site and get optimization recommendations
* **Smart Chatbot** – Add an AI assistant for 24/7 visitor support
* **Audio Content** – Convert posts to audio for podcasts and accessibility

= Why Gen Wave? =

* **No API Keys** – Unlike other AI plugins, you don't need OpenAI or other API keys
* **Simple Setup** – Install, connect your account, and you're ready
* **Pay As You Go** – Use tokens only when you generate content
* **Secure** – All communication is encrypted end-to-end

= Requirements =

* WordPress 5.0 or higher

This plugin communicates with Gen Wave services for authentication and content generation. No data is transmitted without your action. All communication is encrypted.

== Installation ==

**Option 1: Install from WordPress.org**

1. Go to Plugins → Add New in your WordPress admin
2. Search for "Gen Wave"
3. Click "Install Now" and then "Activate"
4. Go to Gen Wave in the admin menu
5. Click "Connect Account" and sign in to your Gen Wave account

**Option 2: Manual Installation**

1. Download the plugin zip file
2. Go to Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin
3. Upload the zip file and click "Install Now"
4. Activate the plugin
5. Go to Gen Wave and connect your account

**After Installation**

Once connected, you can:
* Generate content for any post or product using the "Generate with AI" button
* View your token balance in the Gen Wave dashboard
* Configure your business profile for better AI results

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need API keys from OpenAI or other providers? =

No! That's what makes Gen Wave different. We handle all the AI infrastructure for you. Just connect your Gen Wave account and start generating content.

= Is this plugin free? =

Yes, the Gen Wave plugin is free and includes basic AI content generation for individual posts/products. For bulk generation and advanced features, you'll need [Gen Wave Pro](https://genwave.ai/).

= What's the difference between Free and Pro? =

**Free:** Generate content for one post/product at a time, token balance tracking, multi-language support.

**Pro:** Bulk generation (hundreds of items), AI image generation, SEO analysis, chatbot, audio content conversion.

= How does pricing work? =

Gen Wave uses a token system. You purchase tokens and use them when generating content. Check our [pricing page](https://genwave.ai/) for current rates.

= What languages are supported? =

Gen Wave supports content generation in English, Hebrew, Arabic, Russian, French, and Chinese.

= Can I use Gen Wave with WooCommerce? =

Yes! Gen Wave works great with WooCommerce. Generate product titles, short descriptions, and full descriptions with AI.

= What data is sent to your servers? =

When you generate content, we send the product/post data you want to enhance (title, existing content, etc.) to our AI servers. All data is encrypted. We never store or share your content.

= Is the plugin compatible with multisite? =

Yes, the plugin works on multisite installations. Each site needs its own Gen Wave account connection.
